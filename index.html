A BRIEF OF MYSELF

My name is Nacho Mattos, I moved from Spain three years ago but I'm original from Uruguay.

I'm a nurse graduated by the Medical University of Uruguay. I worked around 10 years as a nurse in a Military Hospital in Montevideo. I have received Military training for 3 months before starting to work in the Hopital. Thanks to this, I have been able to travel and get to know several countries of America. But my biggest trip was to Africa, where I lived in the Democratic Republic of Congo for a year in a humanitarian mission with the UN.

When I returned to Uruguay after having lived that experience I felt that I had to change my life and dedicate myself to what I really love. For that reason, I trained in computer science.


My Interest In Technology And Coding

Solve problems and know that you have created something that will be used productively by another person, I think it is very stimulating and enriching. Coding is one of the easiest ways to solve practical problems. If we consider any other type of engineering, you need some type of raw material: cables, components, large machines and special pieces to create any design you have in your head. But coding only needs a decent computer and Internet connection and the final product depends on how good your idea is.


My Interest in {CODE NATION}

After my first visit to CodeNation, I realized that it is a site that cares about its students. I believe that CodeNation seeks to make great changes in the level of codes education and labor integration in Manchester. It is very motivating to study at Code Nation because I know that this training will have a great impact on my professional career.


Week One

In just one week I learned a lot about the Javascript language, as well as about the use of programs such as "Visual Code" and collaborative platforms such as "Git Hub". The classes have been very well explained and direct to the subject. I think it has been a very enriching week for me and I really want to continue with the next weeks.
