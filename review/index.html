Review the ongoing use of IT tools and change approach as needed

In making your own website, are you happy with the tools you used? E.g. Visual Studio Code, Codepen, Trello

I am very happy using tools such as VSC and Trello, although the latter I would have liked to use it in more depth. I think that a tool like Trello is fundamental to organize the work and be able to distribute the tasks in a team. VSC is a powerful tool that allows you to develop quickly because it is very intuitive and that helps you to optimize the work. Codepen is a very interesting tool that allows you to see in real time the result of your code. In the other, hand and a fundamental characteristic of Code Pen is you can take ideas from other user and reuse code for your own projects

Describe whether the IT tools selected were appropriate

Visual Studio Code, Codepen, Trello

In addition to VSC, Codepen and Trello for me one of the tools that was very useful when developing this project was the Google console. Since with it I could test in real time changes that I wanted to do to later implement it in the code.

Assess the strengths and weaknesses of your final project

Are you happy with your website? Why? Why not?

What would you change?

I am very satisfied with my work although there are always things that can be improved. I am a very critical person and I look at the detail in everything I do, that's why I think that my website lacks a more professional finish, I think with a little more time and perhaps more experience I could finish it better. On the other hand as I have put a form I feel that the back end and how the data management that could be collected would be managed has not been developed.

Describe further improvements you can make to your project

A more professional finish, with images and animations to make the page look more dynamic. I'd like to make content for the user, such as articles written for young developers and a newsletter where you can subscribe.
